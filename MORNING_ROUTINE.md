# Morning Routine (Coffee First)

- 06:30 - Wake up
- 06:32 - Boil water to ~93-95°C
- 06:40 - Pre-warm cup
- 06:45 - Grind coffee (55-60), weigh 15 g for 250 g brew
- 06:50 - Start pour: 70 g, 75 g, 75 g (total 250 g)
- 06:56 - Bloom 45 seconds
- 06:57 - Finish pour, total brew ~3 minutes
- 06:58 - Taste and log in COFFEE_LOG.md
- 07:00 - Begin day

Notes:
- If you want to tweak grind or bloom, log it in COFFEE_LOG.md and I’ll adjust the plan.
